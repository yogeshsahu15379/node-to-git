# Drawer items

The links/buttons that show in the Drawer content slot. See [these comps](https://www.figma.com/file/rgt4xGUPIXGhAzJulrLskn/AES?node-id=2487%3A0).

## Full size

The Drawer Item link fills its container.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Block">
    {() => html`
      <aes-drawer-item
        icon="${IconType.LOGIN}"
        url="http://google.com"
        class="mb-2"
      >
        Login to my account
      </aes-drawer-item>
      <aes-drawer-item
        icon="${IconType.LOGO_RGB}"
        url="http://google.com"
        class="mb-2"
      >
        Ohio
      </aes-drawer-item>
    `}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
