# WYSIWYG styles

The following styles are globally scoped. This means they are exposed to consuming applications for use in WYSIWYG editors.

## Text

## Default text

Body text does not live in a paragraph and just has the basic font and 1rem size.

<Story name="Basic text">
  {() => html`

      ut sapien posuere condimentum. Suspendisse vitae ante est.
    </div>
  `}
</Story>

## Headings

### Default headings (unstyled)

Headings have no styles applied by default.

  {() => html`
    <div class="wysiwyg">

      <h1>Default H3 tag.</h1>
      <h1>Default H4 tag.</h1>
    </div>
  `}
</Story>

## Lists

### Unordered Lists

<Story name="Unordered Lists">
  {() => html`
    <div class="wysiwyg">
      <ul>
        <li>
          Contingency plans. We understand that supply chain plays a key role in
          minimizing the downtime associated with asset failure since unexpected
          high impact events may have a substantial negative impact on the
          business. We have adopted guidelines to create contingency plans
          and/or procedures to identify and respond to incidents and emergency
          situations and maintain continuous operation of critical assets.
          Proper mapping of critical suppliers is included as part of the spend
          analysis activities, and we also identify alternative sources.
        </li>
        <li>
          Inventory & criticality relation. We include criticality assessment
          for the different assets and parts within inventory as well as proper
          planning tools to ensure availability and replenishment according to
          lead times.
        </li>
        <li>
          Asset management. Assessment of supplier performance enables the
          identification of issues to be addressed via corrective action plans
          as well as best practices that should be recognized and duplicated.
        </li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  `}
</Story>
